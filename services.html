Services

Strategic Planning

Any organization is looking to be successful. For this reason, it is essential to trace a clear path that allows reaching the planned goals. However, tracing this path does not only mean establishing the point at which you want to arrive. We help you define what the steps will be to achieve that goal.

Finance evaluation for Investment projects

We can help you financially assess the feasibility of your investment project. With the appropriate information available, we project the return on your investment that will help you define your success.

Due Diligence for Business Acquisitions

Acquisitions of a business require a significant amount of due diligence by the investor. The investor need to knows what it assets is buying and what obligations it is assuming, the nature and extent of the target company’s contingent liabilities, problematic contracts, litigation risks and intellectual property issues, and much more. We have the knowledge to performance the due diligence inquiry to gain all the information you need before buying or invest in a business.

Accounting IFRS

Assistance in the implementation of the International Financial Reporting Standars (IFRS), review of the financial statements to determine what are the required changes in order to comply with the Standards, seeking the reduction of adverse impacts to the financial information.

Accounting USGAAP

Advisory on the correct application of US GAAP, review of the financial statements of subsidiaries located in locations other than US for the purpose of approving financial statements and achieving appropriate consolidated financial statements.

External Audit

Audit performance of financial statements to provide an opinion of an external independent professional valid for interested third party readers.

Internal Audit

Determination of plans and internal audit programs of your organization. Performance of an audit of financial statements.

Cost structure analysis

The cost structure in an organization is essential for the most appropriate decision-making. We help you plan the cost structure of your business, analyze your costs and identify all rationalization opportunities to make your company more profitable. We review the key business processes in order to determine the best cost structure for each of the products or services they sell, essential information to determine if the sale price is appropriate for the viability of the business.

Treasury

Review of financial processes, appropriate use of bank accounts, preparation of cash flow for the purpose of optimizing the use of financial systems and increase business profitability.

​Internal Control

Understanding of the internal processes of the business, understandings of the external elements that affect them, all for the purpose of definitively an appropriate structure of internal control that allows your business to mitigate the risks of losses and also prevent penalties for noncompliance with regulations.
